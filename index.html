Jumbo Lump Crab Cakes

Baltimore Style


Ingredients

  • 1 pound jumbo lump crab meat
  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of old bay seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons of parsely
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon of mustard

Instructions

  1. In large bowl mis bread crumbs, mayo, old bay, parsely, mustard, and egg until well blended. Gently stir in crabmeat. Shape into 4 patties.
  2. Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Fry crab cakes about 5 minutes per side or until cooked through and golden brown. Sprinkle with old bay.
